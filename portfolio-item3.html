Social ImpactManagement Tool

An Impact management system for UNDP Egypt

The main purpose of the Social Impact Management Tool is for the UNDP Egypt to measure the various impact that fund managers, impact management consultants and private companies do in accordance to the 17 SDG's through the collection and aggregation of data on impact funds with a simple tool to effectively conduct impact due diligence reporting on their potential investments.

This Platform was built to cater for multi lingual purposes and aims to empower management consultants to provide investors and businesses with the clarity, insights, and tools required to support and authenticate their contributions to achieving the SDGs in various countries.

Technologies used where Laravel Framework and MYSQL
